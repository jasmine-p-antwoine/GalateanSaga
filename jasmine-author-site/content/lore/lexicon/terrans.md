## Description

The inhabitants of Earth which are citizens of the International Alliance of Earth.

## Contested Term

The inhabitants of Earth, no matter their nation or allegiance. In Galatean culture, they are often viewed as brutal and morally corrupt, while Terran propaganda views Spacers as "monsters" or "machines".

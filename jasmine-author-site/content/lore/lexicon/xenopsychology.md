## Description

A specialized field of science led by Dr. Hayden, dedicated to studying the minds of "non-human" entities. It is used by the IAE to pathologize Spacer behavior and justify their dehumanization.

## Contested Term

Xenopsychology is not recognized as proper science in Galatea.

# AI Policy

Hi friends and fellow creatives,

**AI does not write my books. My novels, stories, and book covers are created by me, without AI-generated text or imagery.**

**However, AI helps me create the immersive multimedia elements of The Galatean Saga universe‚Äîincluding promotional images, soundscapes, music, and video content.**

---

## What AI Does NOT Create

### üìñ My Writing
- All narrative prose, dialogue, and storytelling in my books is written by me
- No AI text generation is used in my published fiction

**üé® Book Covers**
- Book covers are designed by me or commissioned from human artists
- No AI-generated imagery is used for book covers

### What AI DOES Help Create

**üé¨ Multimedia & Promotional Content**
As part of my creative journey‚Äîincluding music, visual storytelling, and video production‚ÄîI use AI as one tool among many to bring The Galatean Saga universe to life beyond the written word.

**My Creative Process:**
1. **AI Generation:** I use AI tools to generate raw material (images, audio, music stems, video clips)
2. **Human Editing & Refinement:** I then edit, remaster, layer, and compile these elements using:
   - Open-source software: GIMP, Inkscape, Audacity, LMMS
   - Professional tools: Filmora, and other editing platforms
   - AI-assisted tools: Suno, Midjourney, ChatGPT, ElevenLabs, Kling AI
3. **Creative Direction:** Every piece reflects my artistic vision, creative choices, and personal touch

The final multimedia content is a collaboration between human creativity and AI assistance‚Äînot raw AI output.

### üõ°Ô∏è My Intentions & Responsibility

- I use these tools to support personal and creative projects, not for deception, exploitation, or violation of licensing
- I actively review and respect the Terms of Use for each platform I use
- If I ever miss a change or unintentionally publish content outside a tool's allowed scope, I will remove or revise the content immediately upon notification
- AI assists my multimedia storytelling‚Äîit does not replace my writing, my voice, or my creative vision

### ü§ù Transparency & Fair Use

- I do not monetize or distribute AI-generated content from tools that limit commercial use
- I give credit where required and avoid presenting generated content as entirely my own if attribution is necessary
- My creative process involves layering, editing, remixing, and refining‚Äînot just accepting raw AI outputs
- The Galatean Saga is an experimental multimedia project where AI tools help me explore storytelling across different mediums (music, visuals, video) while the core narrative remains entirely human-written

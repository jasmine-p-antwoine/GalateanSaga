A New Home for The Galatean Saga

I’m excited to announce the launch of the new official website for The Galatean Saga! After years on Blogger, I’ve moved to a more flexible platform that better reflects the immersive, multi-dimensional nature of this universe.

Why the Change?

The old Blogger site served us well, but as The Galatean Saga has grown from a single book concept into a multimedia storytelling experiment—spanning novels, music, video, and interactive experiences—I needed a space that could evolve with it.

What’s improved:

  • Better Navigation: Find books, multimedia content, and lore more easily
  • Cleaner Design: A modern interface that matches the sci-fi aesthetic of the universe
  • Dedicated Book Pages: Each book gets its own showcase with covers, descriptions, reviews, and purchase links
  • Enhanced Organization: Clear sections for books, blog posts, AI policy, and author information
  • Mobile-Friendly: Fully responsive design that works beautifully on any device

What Stays the Same

My commitment to transparent, ethical storytelling remains unchanged. You can still find my comprehensive AI Policy explaining exactly how I use AI tools in my creative process—and more importantly, what I don’t use them for.

The stories, the characters, the universe—all still crafted with the same care, depth, and human creativity you’ve come to expect.

What’s Next

This new site is just the beginning. Coming soon:

  • More December Chronicles novellas - Additional stories exploring critical moments in Galatean history
  • Edge of Surrender - Book 1 of the main saga (2026)
  • Audiobook for The Spacer - Narrated by Daryl Branson (Early 2026)
  • Interactive Worldbuilding Content - Deep dives into the technology, politics, and cultures of the Galatean universe
  • Behind-the-Scenes Blog Posts - Writing process, character development, and creative insights

Explore the New Site

Take some time to explore:

  • Browse The Books - Start with The Spacer or learn about upcoming releases
  • Read About the Author - Learn about my journey and creative philosophy
  • Connect on Social Media - Join the community across multiple platforms
  • Subscribe to The Galatean Dispatch - Get exclusive updates and content

Thank You

To everyone who’s followed The Galatean Saga from its early days—thank you. Your support, questions, and enthusiasm have helped shape this universe into something far bigger than I initially imagined.

Welcome to the new home. The saga continues.

— Jasmine

AI Policy

Hi friends and fellow creatives,

AI does not write my books. My novels, stories, and book covers are created by me, without AI-generated text or imagery.

However, AI helps me create the immersive multimedia elements of The Galatean Saga universe‚Äîincluding promotional images, soundscapes, music, and video content.

What AI DOES Help Create

üé¨ Multimedia & Promotional Content As part of my creative journey‚Äîincluding music, visual storytelling, and video production‚ÄîI use AI as one tool among many to bring The Galatean Saga universe to life beyond the written word.

My Creative Process:

  1. AI Generation: I use AI tools to generate raw material (images, audio, music stems, video clips)
  2. Human Editing & Refinement: I then edit, remaster, layer, and compile these elements using:
    • Open-source software: GIMP, Inkscape, Audacity, LMMS
    • Professional tools: Filmora, and other editing platforms
    • AI-assisted tools: Suno, Midjourney, ChatGPT, ElevenLabs, Kling AI
  3. Creative Direction: Every piece reflects my artistic vision, creative choices, and personal touch

The final multimedia content is a collaboration between human creativity and AI assistance‚Äînot raw AI output.

  • Kling AI (video generation)

For Human Editing & Production:

  • GIMP, Inkscape (image editing and design)
  • Audacity, LMMS (audio editing and music production)
  • Filmora (video editing and compilation)
